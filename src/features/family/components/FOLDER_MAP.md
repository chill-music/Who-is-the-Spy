# Folder: Family UI Components (src/features/family/components)

## Overview
This directory contains the modular React components that build the comprehensive Family/Tribe dashboard and social experience.

## Communication & Social
- **[FamilyChatModal.js](file:///c:/Users/sheha/OneDrive/Desktop/Who-is-the-Spy-og/src/features/family/components/FamilyChatModal.js)**: A secure, real-time messaging hub for family members. It supports "Red Packet" drops and system-wide news alerts.
- **[RedPacketCard.js](file:///c:/Users/sheha/OneDrive/Desktop/Who-is-the-Spy-og/src/features/family/components/RedPacketCard.js)**: Interactive logic for claiming currency gifts within family chat.
- **[FriendsMomentsModal.js](file:///c:/Users/sheha/OneDrive/Desktop/Who-is-the-Spy-og/src/features/family/components/FriendsMomentsModal.js)**: A curated photo feed showing updates only from designated friends and family members.

## Management & Progression
- **[FamilyProfile.js](file:///c:/Users/sheha/OneDrive/Desktop/Who-is-the-Spy-og/src/features/family/components/FamilyProfile.js)**: The landing page for a family, showing its level, tag, emblem, and total activeness.
- **[FamilyManagement.js](file:///c:/Users/sheha/OneDrive/Desktop/Who-is-the-Spy-og/src/features/family/components/FamilyManagement.js)**: Tools for family admins to change name, emblem, rules, and handle membership applications.
- **[FamilyMembers.js](file:///c:/Users/sheha/OneDrive/Desktop/Who-is-the-Spy-og/src/features/family/components/FamilyMembers.js)**: A scrollable list of all members, showing their role (Leader, Officer, Member) and activeness contribution.
- **[FamilyRoleBadge.js](file:///c:/Users/sheha/OneDrive/Desktop/Who-is-the-Spy-og/src/features/family/components/FamilyRoleBadge.js)**: Small status indicator for family roles.

## Rewards & Games
- **[FamilyGacha.js](file:///c:/Users/sheha/OneDrive/Desktop/Who-is-the-Spy-og/src/features/family/components/FamilyGacha.js)**: A "Velvet" themed loot-box mini-game for spending family points to win exclusive rewards.
- **[FamilyShop.js](file:///c:/Users/sheha/OneDrive/Desktop/Who-is-the-Spy-og/src/features/family/components/FamilyShop.js)**: A specialized store for items restricted to family members.

## Collective Activity
- **[FamilyTreasury.js](file:///c:/Users/sheha/OneDrive/Desktop/Who-is-the-Spy-og/src/features/family/components/FamilyTreasury.js)**: Logic for point donations and financial health of the tribe.
- **[FamilyTasks.js](file:///c:/Users/sheha/OneDrive/Desktop/Who-is-the-Spy-og/src/features/family/components/FamilyTasks.js)**: A list of collective goals (Missions) that all members must contribute to for family experience.
- **[FamilyNews.js](file:///c:/Users/sheha/OneDrive/Desktop/Who-is-the-Spy-og/src/features/family/components/FamilyNews.js)**: A simple activity feed of recent events like joins, leaves, and large donations.

## Discovery & Ranking
- **[FamilySearch.js](file:///c:/Users/sheha/OneDrive/Desktop/Who-is-the-Spy-og/src/features/family/components/FamilySearch.js)**: The portal for finding and applying to join new families.
- **[FamilyRanking.js](file:///c:/Users/sheha/OneDrive/Desktop/Who-is-the-Spy-og/src/features/family/components/FamilyRanking.js)**: Global leaderboards showing the top families by level and total activeness.
