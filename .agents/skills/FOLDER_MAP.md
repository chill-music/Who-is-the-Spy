# Folder: Internal AI Skills (.agents/skills)

## Overview
This directory contains thirty (30) specialized internal skill modules used by the AI to perform complex architectural, design, and project management tasks. These skills are divided into three major categories.

## Categories

### 1. The Speckit Workflow Suite
These skills correspond directly to the automated commands in `.agents/commands/`.
- **Skills**: `speckit-specify`, `speckit-plan`, `speckit-tasks`, `speckit-analyze`, `speckit-implement`, `speckit-checklist`, `speckit-clarify`, `speckit-constitution`, `speckit-taskstoissues`.
- **Purpose**: Managing the end-to-end software development lifecycle from requirements to TDD integration.

### 2. Design System & Frontend Excellence
Focused on the "Cyber-Luxury" aesthetic and technical UI consistency.
- **Foundational**: `frontend-design` (Base components), `normalize` (System alignment), `extract` (Component reuse), `typeset` (Typography), `colorize` (Vibrant palettes).
- **Polish**: `polish` (Quality pass), `harden` (Resilience & Edge cases), `optimize` (Performance & Spacing), `audit` (A11y/Theming checks), `arrange` (Layout rhythm).

### 3. Creative Direction & UX Strategy
Used to adjust the emotional resonance and user experience of a feature.
- **Amplification**: `animate` (Micro-interactions), `bolder` (High-impact styling), `overdrive` (Ambitious animations), `delight` (Personality & Joy).
- **Refining**: `quieter` (Reducing overstimulation), `distill` (Simplification), `clarify` (UX Writing/Microcopy).
- **Discovery**: `onboard` (First-run experiences), `critique` (UX evaluation), `teach-impeccable` (Persisting design context).
