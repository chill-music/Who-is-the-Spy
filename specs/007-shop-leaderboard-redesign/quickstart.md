# Quickstart: 007-shop-leaderboard-redesign

## Verification Steps
1.  **Open Shop**: Open the ShopModal and verify the grid items match the `.shop-standard-item` style.
2.  **Test Rarity Labels**: Verify that Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, and Mythic items have their respective glowing border styles.
3.  **Check Portrait Effects**: Navigate to the `profileEffects` tab in the Shop and verify that the animated effects display in a 9:16 aspect ratio container.
4.  **Confirm Podium**: Open the Leaderboard (Wins/Charisma/Family) and verify that the Top 3 players are side-by-side with their respective podium frame assets.
5.  **Responsive Check**: Verify that the grid and podium scale correctly on mobile view.
