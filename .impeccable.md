## Design Context

### Users
 elite social gamers looking for status, connection, and a competitive feel. Goal: Feel like a high-status VIP with deep social connections (Couples, BFFs, Families).

### Brand Personality
**Luxurious, Competitive, Social.** 
The voice is premium and exclusive. Every interaction should make the user feel like they are part of an elite "Covert Arena" or exclusive social club.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark premium "Mobile Legends" style. It relies on deep backgrounds (#05050a to #1a1a2e), glassmorphism (20px blur), and high-contrast neon accents (Cyan #00f2ff, Purple #7000ff). Glow effects and shimmer animations (like the VIP 10 streak) are core to the identity.

**IMPORTANT:** The existing design identity must be preserved. We focus on polish, responsiveness, and bug fixing, not redesign.

### Design Principles
1. **Elite Status First**: Reinforce user status through badges, glowing levels, and premium effects.
2. **High-Contrast Neon**: Maintain sharp legibility with vibrant colors against deep dark backgrounds.
3. **Glassy & Glossy**: Use translucency and light-refracting effects to communicate a "premium" surface.
4. **Social Connection**: Prioritize social interaction points (BFF, Marriage/CP, Family tags) through distinctive visual cues.
5. **Functional Precision**: Ensure perfect alignment and responsiveness across all devices (Mobile-First focus).
